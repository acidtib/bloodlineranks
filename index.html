our legacy

founded in 1896 during the height of the bayou's blood-soaked turmoil, bloodline ranks was born out of necessity.

our founders, acidtib and tsauron, were seasoned hunters turned craftsmen, who realized that many hunters fell not to monsters but to their own lack of preparation and tools.

acidtib

brought a craftsman's precision to the endeavor, creating tools grounded in meticulous engineering and field testing.

tsauron

known for his strategic acumen and mastery of tactical resourcefulness, provided the innovative concepts and foresight needed to ensure hunters were always one step ahead of their prey.

while others focus on brute force—arming hunters with firearms and explosives—bloodline ranks believes that survival lies in preparation, knowledge, and precision.
